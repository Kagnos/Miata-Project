Project Street Ronin

This website is my outline and plan for my next car. This project has two main goals:

1. Learn what it means to drive a car. I want this car to be an extension of myself. It will teach me the importance of shifting, braking, steering, handling, listening to your car, etc.

2. Slowly build the car into a unique build as an expression of myself. This car will be dark, subtle, focused, and uniquely mine.

1. Base Platform
  • 2001 – 2003 Miata MX-5 LS or SE trim (comes with a torsen LSD and better suspension)
  • Hardtop if possible, or will buy one later
  • Manual transmission ONLY
  • Black or Titanuim Gray factory paint
2. Wheels & Tires
  • Konig Hexaform wheels in black or a gunmetal/graphite(15x8 +25) are my favorite for a budget wheel
3. Suspension
4. Interior
  • Custom gauges from revlimiter, black background and white contrasting against deep purple or red for details
  • Custom bucket seats, black with purple stitching
  • Aftermarket steering wheel
  • Weighted shift knob
  • Black Hard Dog roll bar
  • Pedal covers?
5. Sound
6. Exterior
  • OEM hardtop
  • Spoiler
  • Front lip
  • Smoked headlights
  • Smoked taillights
7. Extras
  • 35% ceramic tint on sides and rear or 90's JDM style blue-gray tint
  • Debadge front and rear
  • Simple and clean: no stickers
