Project Street Ronin


This is my outline and plan for my next car. This project has two main goals:

1. Learn what it means to drive a car. I want this car to be an extension of myself. It will teach me the importance of shifting, braking, steering, driving feel, listening to your car, etc.

2. Slowly build the car into a unique build as an expression of myself. This car will be dark, subtle, focused, and uniquely mine.

1. Base Platform
  • 2001 – 2003 LS or SE trim (comes with a torsen LSD and better suspension)
  • Hardtop if possible, or will buy one later
  • Manual transmission ONLY
  • Color? Preferably black, titanium gray, or dark blue
2. Wheels & Tires
  • Konig Hexaform wheels in black or a gunmetal/graphite(15x8 +25)
3. Suspension
4. Interior
5. Sound
6. Exterior

  • Spoiler
